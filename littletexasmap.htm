Directions to Little Texas Ranch
Take Route 17 to exit 104 (Monticello Raceway exit). Take Route 17B past the Racetrack and into Hortonville. Turn right onto CR 121 (just past the Ford dealership) into North Branch. Continue straight onto CR 95 into Obernburg.
County Road 95 runs from North Branch (where CR 121 ends) into Fremont Center (where CR 94 begins). Obernburg Road leads to Roscoe. Obernburg is roughly 10 miles from Roscoe, 10 miles from Jeffersonville and 10 miles from Callicoon! (About 2 1/2 hours from New York City)
